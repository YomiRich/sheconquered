Crystal Muyakane

Tell us about yourself


My name is Crystal Muyakane. I am a digital community manager, a communications professional, public speaker and certified professional life coach (CPLC), I focus on personal development, storytelling, and tribe building. I received my Bachelor of Arts in Communications/Journalism at The University Of Nairobi and my Masters in Strategic Organizational Communication at Rutgers University.


How did you manage postpartum depression?


I got pregnant at 20 years old. It was an unplanned pregnancy. Before I gave birth the father of my son had already denied responsibility. Therefore, I had to do everything by myself. It was stressful for me because I had just joined campus. I had a relationship with the nurse that was assigned to me. So she advised me to go through the She conquered website. After going through the website I was in utter disbelief because, I did not know so many people are/ have gone through what I was facing. It was a relief to know that there was this community of women that had my back. I was dedicated to the plan, when things got tough, I called Yomi or just vent/ask questions on the Queendom. I visited the site every single day and attended all the mobile clinics. I got to understand that I could still go ahead and achieve my goals as if nothing happened. I loved my son more. I have grown as an individual, I am more confident, energetic...I am goals!!(laughs).


How long did it take for you to recover?


It took me around four months.


Woow... Thats really beautiful!!What advice would you give a young mum?


You are a woman first, then a mother. Your circumstances do not define you. Whether you are single mom or not, married or not, it is tough raising a child and at the same time pursue your goals. You will come home with assignments, your husband and children are waiting for you...Girl, keep at it. Do not stop. Maximize your hours a day, be consistent at meeting the daily targets you set for yourself, grow every single day. Talk to a friend, or post on Queendom what you are going through. The women there are going to pray with you, and ensure that you are on your feet.


Your energy is tangible!.What is next for Crystal?


I am organizing a campaign that is geared at helping girls between 13-23 years old know their identity and teach them how they can pursue their purpose. The events will be held quartely with the first edition being held in Kilifi County mid October to the last week of November.

Thank you so much for your time Crystal. You are increased and favoured in all that you do.
